Introduction to Machine Learning Webinar

October 2020

For RoadBotics, in order to help the customers understand the technology we were using to grade their roads, I gave this presentation to provide a basic foundation of machine learning, with a slight focus on deep learning. Topics covered are:

  1. What is machine learning?
  2. How models make choices: Regression vs Classification
  3. What makes a model a deep learning model?
  4. How do deep learning models “learn”?
  5. Advanced techniques used to train models
  6. What mistakes do models make and how do we evaluate them?
